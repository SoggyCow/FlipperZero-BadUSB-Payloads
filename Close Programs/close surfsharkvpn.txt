REM Title: Close Surfshark VPN Processes
REM Author: SoggyCow
REM Description: Opens elevated CMD to forcefully terminate Surfshark VPN processes (Surfshark.exe and Surfshark.Service.exe) and closes the terminal

DELAY 1000
GUI r
DELAY 500
STRING cmd
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "Surfshark.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "Surfshark.Service.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING exit
ENTER
