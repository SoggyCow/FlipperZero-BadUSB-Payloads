REM Title: Close ExpressVPN Processes
REM Author: SoggyCow
REM Description: Opens elevated CMD to forcefully terminate ExpressVPN processes (expressvpn-ui.exe and expressvpn.exe) and closes the terminal

DELAY 1000
GUI r
DELAY 500
STRING cmd
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "expressvpn-ui.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "expressvpn.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING exit
ENTER
