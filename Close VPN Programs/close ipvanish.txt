REM Title: Close IPVanish VPN Processes
REM Author: SoggyCow
REM Description: Opens elevated CMD to forcefully terminate IPVanish VPN processes (IPVanish.exe and ipvanish-service.exe) and closes the terminal

DELAY 1000
GUI r
DELAY 500
STRING cmd
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "IPVanish.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "ipvanish-service.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING exit
ENTER
