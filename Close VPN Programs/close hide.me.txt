REM Title: Close Hide.me VPN Processes
REM Author: SoggyCow
REM Description: Opens elevated CMD to forcefully terminate Hide.me VPN processes (hide.me.exe and hidemevpn.exe) and closes the terminal

DELAY 1000
GUI r
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "hide.me.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "hidemevpn.exe" /F
ENTER
