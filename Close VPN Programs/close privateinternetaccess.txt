REM Title: Close Private Internet Access (PIA) VPN Processes
REM Author: SoggyCow
REM Description: Opens elevated CMD to forcefully terminate PIA VPN processes (pia-client.exe and pia-service.exe) and closes the terminal

DELAY 1000
GUI r
DELAY 500
STRING cmd
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "pia-client.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "pia-service.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING exit
ENTER
