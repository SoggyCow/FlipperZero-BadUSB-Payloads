REM Title: Close PrivateVPN Processes
REM Author: SoggyCow
REM Description: Opens elevated CMD to forcefully terminate PrivateVPN processes (PrivateVPN.exe and PrivateVPNService.exe) and closes the terminal

DELAY 1000
GUI r
DELAY 500
STRING cmd
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "PrivateVPN.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "PrivateVPNService.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING exit
ENTER
