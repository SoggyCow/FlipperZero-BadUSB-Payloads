REM Title: Close ProtonVPN Processes
REM Author: SoggyCow
REM Description: Opens elevated CMD to forcefully terminate ProtonVPN processes (ProtonVPN.exe and ProtonVPNService.exe) and closes the terminal

DELAY 1000
GUI r
DELAY 500
STRING cmd
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "ProtonVPN.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "ProtonVPNService.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING exit
ENTER
