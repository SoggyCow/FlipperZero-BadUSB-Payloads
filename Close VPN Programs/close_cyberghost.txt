REM Title: Close CyberGhost VPN Processes
REM Author: SoggyCow
REM Description: Opens elevated CMD to forcefully terminate CyberGhost VPN processes (CyberGhost.exe and CyberGhost.Service.exe) and closes the terminal

DELAY 1000
GUI r
DELAY 500
STRING cmd
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "CyberGhost.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "CyberGhost.Service.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING exit
ENTER
