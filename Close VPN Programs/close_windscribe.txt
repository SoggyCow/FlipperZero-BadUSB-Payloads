REM Title: Close Windscribe VPN Processes
REM Author: SoggyCow
REM Description: Opens elevated CMD to forcefully terminate Windscribe VPN processes (Windscribe.exe and WindscribeService.exe) and closes the terminal

DELAY 1000
GUI r
DELAY 500
STRING cmd
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "Windscribe.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING taskkill /IM "WindscribeService.exe" /F
ENTER
DELAY 500
STRING exit
ENTER
